Batting is one of the important part of cricket.  In batting,  the bowl thrown by the bowler is hitted by the batsman. If the bowl touches the boundary with contact to ground the batting team will be awarded 4 runs and if the bowl touches the boundry without contact to ground. Batting team will be awarded 6 runs.


There are many rules in batting. Some of them are:-


  1. Player should not walk in middle of pitch
  2. Batsman should not disturb the fielders
  3. Batsman should not hit the bowl twice with the bat
  4. Batsman showld fight with umpire about his decision
  5. Batsman should respect bowlers and fielders

Some important things to know to be a batsman

  • Grip
  • Stance

Grip

Grip is the way in which batsman holds the bat. Grip is very important in cricket. Its is essential that both the the grip should be of 'V' shape.

Stance

Stance is the position of a batsman while batting. Stance is also very much important in cricket.The batsman's head should be facing the bowler.

There are different types of shots in batting in cricket. Some of them are :-

  1. cut
  2. slice
  3. cover drive
  4. hook
  5. pull
  6. sweep
  7. reverse sweep
  8. upper cut
  9. helicopter shot
  10. scoop

There are many other shots in cricket. The knowledge of shots are also very important in cricket.

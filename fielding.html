Fielding is the process in which fielders try to stop batsmen in making runs. Fielding is very important in cricket. If bowl is caught by the fielder befor the contact to ground the batsman will be out.


Some important things to know in fielding are:-

  1. Catching a bowl
  2. Throwing a bowl accurately

Catch a bowl 

It is really important for a fielder to catch a bowl. One mistake in fielding can cause a lose for the team. Fielder should be focused and should be ready to catch bowl any every second in the match.

Throwing a bowl accurately

Throwing a ball accurately is very important in fielding. It helps in securing runs for the team. If a fielder does not know to throw the bowl accurately it will be difficult for the team to win the match.


There are different rules for fielding in cricket. Some of them are:-

  1. Fielders are not allowed to be on the pitch before batsman hit the bowl
  2. Fielders should not hit the batsman or bowler

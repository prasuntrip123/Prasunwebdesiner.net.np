Bowling is the process of throwing bowl in cricket. There are two types of bowlers. They are mainly  Fast Bowler and Spinners.


Fast Bowlers

Fast bowlers  is a bowler who bowls in high pace. Fast Bowlers are very important in cricket. 

Spinners

There are two types of spinners leg spinners and off spinners. There are very less difference between leg spinners and off spinners. 


Rules in bowling

There are various rules while bowling. Some of them are:-

  1. Bowlers are not allowed to cross the crease while bowling. 
  2. Bowlers are not allowed to bowl more that 2 bouncers in ODI and T20 
  3. Bowlers should be fare with batsmans 

Important things to know in bowling

  1. Variation
  2. Accuracy

Variation

Variation is very important for a bowler while bowling. Variation is the change in a particular type of bowl. It helps to take wicket.

Accuracy

Accuracy is the right bowling length and line which creates difficulty for batsman to make runs. It is also very important in cricket
